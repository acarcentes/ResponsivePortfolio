About Me

It's the inspired student that continues to learn on their own what separates the real achievers in the world from those who pedal along, finishing assignments. Science is a cooperative enterprise, spanning the generations. It's the passing of a torch from teacher, to student, to teacher. A community of minds reaching back to antiquity and forward to the stars. Curious that we spend more time congratulating people who have succeeded than encouraging people who have not.
